Install Veltro

Safari Manual Installation (Most Reliable)

Installation Steps
1
📱
Open Veltro in Safari
Make sure you're using the Safari browser (not Chrome or other browsers)
2
⎋
Tap the Share button
Look for the Share button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
3
📜
Scroll down and find "Add to Home Screen"
Swipe up through the options until you see "Add to Home Screen"
4
✏️
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
You'll see a preview of how the icon will look on your home screen
5
➕
Tap "Add" in the top right corner
The Veltro icon will be added to your home screen
✓
🎉
Installation Complete!
Look for the Veltro icon on your home screen and tap it to open
✅ Why This Method Works Best:
• Built-in iOS feature (most reliable)
• No additional files or downloads
• Works on all iOS versions
• No profile installation required
• Custom Veltro icon included

⚠️ Troubleshooting

Can't find Share button? - Make sure you're scrolled to the top of the page

Don't see "Add to Home Screen"? - Scroll further down through the share options

Icon looks wrong? - iOS may use default icon, but Veltro will still work perfectly

Opens in browser? - This is normal for web apps, Veltro will still work great
