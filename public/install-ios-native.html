How to Add Veltro
Follow these simple steps to add Veltro to your home screen
1
Tap the Share button
Look for the share icon ⎋ at the bottom of your screen
2
Scroll down
Find and tap "Add to Home Screen" in the share menu
3
Tap "Add"
Confirm by tapping "Add" in the top right corner
✓
Done!
Veltro icon now appears on your home screen
💡 Tips
• Make sure you're using Safari for the best experience
• The share button is usually at the bottom center of the screen
• You can rearrange the icon after adding it to your home screen
