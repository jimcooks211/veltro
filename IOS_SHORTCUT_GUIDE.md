# Veltro iOS Shortcut Installation Guide

## 🎯 What is the Veltro iOS Shortcut?

The Veltro iOS Shortcut is a custom shortcut that adds a Veltro icon to your iPhone home screen. When you tap the icon, it opens Veltro instantly, giving you a native app-like experience without needing to download anything from the App Store.

## ✨ Benefits

- **Custom Veltro icon** on your home screen
- **One-tap access** to Veltro
- **Full-screen experience** (no browser bars)
- **Works offline** (cached content)
- **No App Store** required
- **Free to install**

## 📱 Installation Steps

### Step 1: Get the Shortcut

1. Visit the installation page: `https://veltroinvestment.vercel.app/install-shortcut.html`
2. Tap the **"Get Veltro Shortcut"** button
3. The Shortcuts app will open automatically

### Step 2: Add the Shortcut

1. In the Shortcuts app, you'll see the Veltro shortcut
2. Tap **"Add Shortcut"** in the top right corner
3. The shortcut will be added to your Shortcuts library

### Step 3: Add to Home Screen

1. Tap the **"..."** (three dots) on the Veltro shortcut
2. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. You'll see a preview of how the icon will look
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner
5. Position the icon on your home screen
6. Tap **"Add"** to confirm

### Step 4: Done!

The Veltro icon now appears on your home screen. Tap it anytime to open Veltro instantly!

## 🎨 Customization Options

### Change the Icon

1. Tap the **"..."** on the Veltro shortcut
2. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. Tap the icon preview
4. Choose from:
   - **Photo** - Use your own image
   - **File** - Use a saved image
   - **Emoji** - Use an emoji as the icon
5. Tap **"Choose"** and select your preferred icon

### Change the Name

1. Tap the **"..."** on the Veltro shortcut
2. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. Edit the name field
4. Enter your preferred name (e.g., "Veltro", "Investments", "Trading")
5. Tap **"Add"**

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Shortcut doesn't open automatically

**Problem:** Tapping "Get Veltro Shortcut" doesn't open Shortcuts app

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)
2. Check that JavaScript is enabled in Safari
3. Try refreshing the page and tapping again
4. Manually open the Shortcuts app and look for the shortcut

### Can't find the shortcut after adding

**Problem:** Added shortcut but can't find it in Shortcuts app

**Solutions:**
1. Open the Shortcuts app
2. Tap the **"Shortcuts"** tab at the bottom
3. Look for "Veltro Investment" in your shortcuts list
4. Use the search bar to find it

### "Add to Home Screen" option missing

**Problem:** Don't see "Add to Home Screen" option

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you're running iOS 13 or later
2. Update your iOS device to the latest version
3. Restart your device and try again

### Icon doesn't appear on home screen

**Problem:** Added to home screen but icon doesn't show up

**Solutions:**
1. Check other home screen pages (swipe left/right)
2. Look in the App Library (swipe left past last home screen)
3. Restart your device
4. Try adding the shortcut again

### Shortcut opens in browser instead of full screen

**Problem:** Tapping icon opens Safari with browser bars

**Solutions:**
1. This is normal behavior for web shortcuts
2. Veltro will still work perfectly
3. For full-screen experience, use Safari's "Add to Home Screen" method instead
